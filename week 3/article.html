Serious accident in Debrecen: Tram hits a cyclist

On March 4, 2026, at 10:44 a.m., a serious traffic accident occurred in Debrecen: a tram struck a cyclist on Thomas Mann Street, near Károlyi Mihály Street. The city’s professional firefighters were dispatched to the scene, and a crane was also requested to assist with the rescue. Technical rescue operations are being coordinated by the disaster management operations service. Unfortunately, the cyclist died at the scene.

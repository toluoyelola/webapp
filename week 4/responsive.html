Things you should know before visiting a thrift store

Items in a thrift store are usually significantly below market value, because they have been used. Regardless of that, they are in quite good condition. This combination of low prices and good conditions might lead to the urge to overconsume, but consumers do not consider this overconsumption because the prices do not reflect a lot of spending that is usually indicative of overconsumption. I hope I have brought this to the forfront of your mind, so that your are more mindful on your next trip to the thrift store
